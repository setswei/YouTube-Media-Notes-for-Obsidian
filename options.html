YouTube Media Notes for Obsidian

Obsidian Vault Settings

Configure how your notes are saved to Obsidian

Leave empty to use the default vault. This should match exactly the name of your Obsidian vault.

Note Location

Configure where your notes are stored within your vault

The folder path within your vault where notes will be saved. Leave empty to save in the vault root.

Note Format

Configure how your notes are formatted

Text to prepend to the note title.

Tags to include in your notes.

Available variables:

  • {{url}} - The YouTube URL with timestamp
  • {{title}} - The video title
  • {{tags}} - Your custom tags
  • {{timestamp}} - The current timestamp in seconds

Note: Timestamps from the video description will be automatically added to the end of the note if they exist.

Time to wait before closing the Obsidian URL tab (in milliseconds). Default: 2500
